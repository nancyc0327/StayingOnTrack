By 3 Months Old, My Child Will Likely...

Physical Development:

  • Can see, hear, feel, taste and smell (at birth).
  • Use reflexes.
  • Focus eyes.
  • Eat every 3 to 4 hours.
  • Lift head and chest when laying on stomach.
  • Follow a moving object or person with his eyes.
  • Grasp a rattle or a finger.
  • Wiggle and kick his legs.

Social & Emotional Development:

  • Like soft, high-pitched voices.
  • Like to look at faces.
  • Startle at loud sounds.
  • Smile.
  • React to distress by crying.
  • Make crying, cooing and grunting sounds.
  • Cry in different ways to express meaning.

Intellectual Development:

  • Recognize mother or primary caregiver.
  • Follow light or objects with eyes.
  • Recognize bottle or breast.
  • Stare and smile at faces.

Delevopmental Milestones Associated with Feeding:

  • Have sucking, rooting and swallowing reflexes.
  • Feel hunger and indicates desire for food by crying; expresses satiety by falling asleep.
  • Have strong extrusion reflex (to thrust out).
  • Begin to develop hand-eye coordination.
  • Approximate lips to the rim of a cup.
